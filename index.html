What is B-Pulse?

A proven medical technology, 100% non-invasive

B-Pulse is a chair that uses PEMF (pulsed electromagnetic field) technology, used in medicine for over 40 years, to deeply activate and strengthen the pelvic floor muscles.

In just 30 minutes, your muscles perform the equivalent of about 13,000 deep contractions — like months of Kegel exercises, but effortless and pain-free.
